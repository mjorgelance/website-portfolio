WrestleQuest

WrestleQuest is a unique fusion of turn-based RPG mechanics and professional wrestling, where players rise from rookie to legend in an epic wrestling journey. Featuring strategic battles, dynamic arenas, and larger-than-life characters, the game blends nostalgic pixel-art aesthetics with modern cinematic storytelling.

As a core contributor to the development team, I was responsible for level design, cinematic system architecture, and interactive progression mechanics, ensuring a seamless and immersive player experience. My contributions include:

  • Level Design & Cinematic Lead: Designed engaging, thematic arenas and narrative-driven environments, integrating story progression seamlessly into gameplay.
  • Cinematic System Development & Implementation: Contributed to the core cinematic system, implementing event-driven cutscenes and dynamic camera transitions to enhance storytelling.
  • Cinematic Routine Implementation: Developed and scripted almost all in-game cinematics, ensuring fluid animation sequencing, event-driven interactions, and engaging narrative moments.
  • Quest Progression & Interactive Minigames: Designed and implemented cinematic progression quests, where players must complete specific challenges before advancing the storyline.
  • Event-Driven Minigame Mechanics: Integrated interactive mechanics using event handlers, creating minigame-like sequences tied to story progression and environmental interactions.
  • Gameplay & Interactable Systems: Developed quest-based interactive elements, allowing players to engage with objects, NPCs, and the environment to trigger unique wrestling-inspired events.

By contributing to both the cinematic and gameplay systems, this project showcases my expertise in level design, cinematic scripting, event-driven interactions, and quest progression mechanics. My work on dynamic cutscenes, interactive minigames, and story-driven gameplay systems highlights my ability to integrate narrative elements seamlessly into core mechanics, ensuring an immersive, strategic, and engaging wrestling-inspired RPG experience.

Idle Clicker

Idle Clicker is a resource management and incremental simulation game where players build and expand their production empire. With engaging idle mechanics, players automate production, manage upgrades, prestige systems, and optimize efficiency to maximize profits.

I am responsible for the entire game development process, from game system architecture and UI/UX design to progression mechanics and player retention strategies. Key features include:

  • Scalable Game Economy & Incremental Revenue System: Designed a dynamic economy that balances passive income generation, automation, and scaling difficulty.
  • Automated Production & Prestige Mechanics: Implemented idle earnings, collector upgrades, and reset-based prestige rewards to enhance long-term engagement.
  • Cloud Save Integration: Developed cross-platform saving using Cloud Save, ensuring players can resume progress across devices.
  • Google AdMob Monetization: Integrated rewarded ads, banner ads, and interstitial ads, optimizing for non-intrusive player engagement while maximizing ad revenue.
  • Intuitive & Dynamic UI/UX: Designed seamless and accessible UI components, including interactive buttons, progress bars, and visually engaging feedback systems.
  • Optimized Performance & Game Balance: Ensured smooth gameplay by implementing object pooling, memory optimization, and balanced scaling mechanics.

This project showcases my expertise in incremental game design, data persistence, monetization strategies, and scalable architecture.

Songbook App

Songbook App is a dynamic tool built for musicians, worship leaders, and music teams. It includes rich chord-based functionalities, transposition controls, and font scaling to suit live performance needs. Users can import personal songs locally or through the cloud, and even submit entries for administrative approval with detailed notes.

  • Implemented real-time transposing and chord formatting for songs.
  • Integrated cloud and local storage with Firebase for persistent song data.
  • Built submission workflows for personal song uploads with admin review, notes, and approval/rejection logic.
  • Included global vs. private song toggles, fullscreen lyric views, and dynamic font controls.
  • Designed a responsive UI suitable for both desktop and mobile musicians.

This project highlights my capabilities in music-focused web app design, cloud integration, real-time UI controls, and administrative content management features.
